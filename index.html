Concert Frames

Melody in Motion

Sound isn’t static—these layered visuals move with you as you scroll, giving each beat depth and motion. Think of it like watching the waveform bloom: the top layer pulses, the underlayer hums, and the third rides the rhythm.

From Stage to Skyline: The first layer drops you inside the venue—lights flaring, bass thumping. Beneath it, the second captures the crowd’s roar and the sea of movement. The third layer pulls back to reveal the city that hosted the night, glowing with post-show energy. Together, they tell the full story of a concert—before, during, and after the last note fades.
